Our Services

Specialists in human resources, administration, accounting, consulting, translations, auditing and training.

Human Resources Management

  • Migration Support: Visa acquisition, DIRE, Work Permit, Employment Authorizations, and other documents.
  • Labor Mediation: Employee placement, interviews, recruitment, payroll processing, and related services.

Administration

  • Company registration
  • Payroll processing
  • Funds transfer
  • Employee roster & work hours management
  • INSS social security procedures
  • Registration in SISSMO
  • Tax payments and related services

Accounting

  • Records for assets, liabilities and equity; Income Statements, VAT, inventory, and closings.
  • Security of financial information via regular database backups.
  • Specific financial reports through collection, analysis and synthesis of accounting data and trends.

Consulting (SHEQ)

  • Creation of SHEQ files and implementation of the Management System.
  • Guidance on gap analyses and SHE inspections.
  • OHS services to ensure safe and welcoming workplaces.

Official Translation & Interpreting

  • Professional EN↔PT translation across document formats.
  • Rental of equipment for simultaneous and consecutive interpretation.

Training
